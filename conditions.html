Upper Body Conditions

Shoulder, neck, and upper back issues

Shoulder Pain & Impingement

Common Symptoms:

  • Pain when reaching overhead
  • Difficulty sleeping on affected side
  • Weakness in shoulder movements
  • Clicking or popping sensations

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Targeted release of rotator cuff muscles, reduction of inflammation, restoration of proper shoulder mechanics, and improvement of range of motion through manual therapy techniques.

Neck Tension & Headaches

Common Symptoms:

  • Tension headaches
  • Stiffness and reduced neck mobility
  • Pain radiating to shoulders
  • Difficulty turning head

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Release of tight neck muscles, reduction of trigger points, improvement of posture, and relief from tension-type headaches through targeted manual therapy.

Upper Back Stiffness

Common Symptoms:

  • Tightness between shoulder blades
  • Difficulty with deep breathing
  • Pain with prolonged sitting
  • Reduced thoracic mobility

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Deep tissue work on thoracic muscles, myofascial release, postural correction strategies, and mobility enhancement for improved upper back function.

Rotator Cuff Issues

Common Symptoms:

  • Pain with overhead activities
  • Weakness in shoulder rotation
  • Night pain
  • Limited range of motion

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Targeted treatment of rotator cuff muscles, reduction of compensatory patterns, strengthening support through manual therapy, and progressive rehabilitation protocols.

Tennis/Golfer's Elbow

Common Symptoms:

  • Pain on outside (tennis) or inside (golfer's) of elbow
  • Weakness in grip strength
  • Pain with wrist movements
  • Difficulty with daily activities

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Treatment of forearm muscles, reduction of tendon inflammation, breaking down scar tissue, and restoration of normal muscle function through specialized techniques.

Lower Body Conditions

Hip, knee, and ankle issues

Hip Mobility Restrictions

Common Symptoms:

  • Limited hip flexion or extension
  • Difficulty with squatting
  • Tightness in hip flexors or glutes
  • Compensatory lower back pain

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Release of hip flexors, glutes, and surrounding muscles, improvement of hip range of motion, correction of movement patterns, and reduction of compensatory stress.

Knee Pain & Tracking Issues

Common Symptoms:

  • Pain around or behind kneecap
  • Clicking or grinding sensations
  • Pain with stairs or squatting
  • Swelling after activity

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Treatment of quadriceps, hamstrings, and calf muscles, improvement of patellar tracking, reduction of muscle imbalances, and support for proper knee mechanics.

ITB Syndrome

Common Symptoms:

  • Pain on outside of knee
  • Worse with running or cycling
  • Tightness along outer thigh
  • Pain increases with distance

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Release of IT band and associated muscles, treatment of hip abductors, correction of running mechanics, and progressive return to activity protocols.

Ankle Injuries

Common Symptoms:

  • Reduced ankle mobility
  • Chronic instability
  • Scar tissue from previous sprains
  • Difficulty with balance

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Breaking down scar tissue, restoration of ankle mobility, treatment of calf muscles, and support for proprioception and stability improvement.

Plantar Fasciitis

Common Symptoms:

  • Heel pain, especially in morning
  • Pain after prolonged standing
  • Tenderness in arch of foot
  • Difficulty walking barefoot

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Treatment of calf muscles and plantar fascia, reduction of inflammation, improvement of foot mechanics, and integration with stretching protocols.

Core & Spine Conditions

Back pain and postural issues

Lower Back Pain

Common Symptoms:

  • Dull ache or sharp pain in lower back
  • Stiffness in morning
  • Pain with bending or lifting
  • Difficulty standing for long periods

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Release of paraspinal muscles, treatment of hip flexors and glutes, improvement of core stability, and reduction of muscle spasms through targeted manual therapy.

Postural Dysfunction

Common Symptoms:

  • Forward head posture
  • Rounded shoulders
  • Chronic muscle tension
  • Fatigue from poor alignment

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Corrective massage for overactive muscles, activation of underactive muscles, postural education, and progressive improvement of alignment through consistent treatment.

Sciatica

Common Symptoms:

  • Pain radiating down leg
  • Numbness or tingling
  • Weakness in leg or foot
  • Pain worse with sitting

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Treatment of piriformis and deep hip rotators, reduction of nerve compression, improvement of lower back mobility, and coordination with other healthcare providers.

Disc-Related Issues

Common Symptoms:

  • Localized back pain
  • Possible radiating pain
  • Muscle guarding and spasm
  • Limited range of motion

How Massage Therapy Helps:

Supportive treatment to reduce muscle tension, improvement of surrounding muscle function, pain management, and coordination with medical treatment plans.

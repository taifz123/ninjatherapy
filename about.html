About Yuhan

As a certified sports and remedial massage therapist, I've dedicated my career to helping people overcome pain and achieve their physical goals. Whether you're an athlete pushing for personal bests or someone dealing with everyday aches and pains, I'm here to help you move better and feel stronger.

My approach combines advanced manual therapy techniques with a deep understanding of human movement and biomechanics. I don't believe in one-size-fits-all treatments - every session is tailored to your unique needs, goals, and body.

What sets my practice apart is the holistic approach. I focus on addressing the root cause of your issues, not just the symptoms, ensuring you receive comprehensive care that promotes long-term healing and performance.

Our Mission

Ninja Motion Therapies was founded on the goal of helping people get back on their feet and be independent in their everyday lives with a functional body to drive them closer to their personal achievements.

Whether you're recovering from injury, managing chronic pain, or optimizing athletic performance, my mission is to empower you with the tools, knowledge, and treatment you need to take control of your body's health and performance.

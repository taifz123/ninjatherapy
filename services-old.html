What to Expect During Your Session

Before Your Session

  • Arrive 5-10 minutes early for your first visit to complete intake forms
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Avoid eating a heavy meal immediately before your session
  • Bring any relevant medical information or imaging results
  • Come prepared to discuss your goals and concerns

During Your Session

  • Initial consultation to discuss your condition and goals (first visit)
  • Physical assessment of affected areas and movement patterns
  • Hands-on treatment using appropriate techniques for your needs
  • Communication throughout -let me know if pressure needs adjustment
  • Education about your condition and self-care strategies

After Your Session

  • Drink plenty of water to help flush out released toxins
  • You may experience some soreness for 24-48 hours (normal response)
  • Follow any self-care recommendations provided
  • Avoid intense training immediately after deep tissue work
  • Schedule your next session based on your treatment plan

Need an Urgent Appointment?

If you have an acute injury or urgent need for treatment, please call us directly. We do our best to accommodate same-day appointments for urgent cases.

Call Now: 0434 766 414

For serious injuries, please seek medical attention first. We can support your recovery once you have medical clearance.

What to Bring to Your Appointment

First Visit

  • Photo ID
  • Health fund card (if claiming)
  • Any relevant medical reports or imaging
  • List of current medications
  • Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Arrive 10 minutes early for paperwork

Follow-Up Visits

  • Comfortable clothing for treatment
  • Water bottle (stay hydrated)
  • Any new medical information
  • Questions or concerns to discuss
  • Arrive 5 minutes early

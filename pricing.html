Which Session Length is Right for You?

Not sure which package to choose? Here's a guide

Choose 30 Minutes If:

  • You're an existing client
  • You need targeted work on one specific area
  • You're maintaining results from previous sessions
  • You want a quick recovery boost between training
  • You have limited time available

Choose 60 Minutes If:

  • This is your first visit (recommended)
  • You need comprehensive assessment and treatment
  • You have multiple related problem areas
  • You want a full treatment session
  • You're not sure which length to choose

Choose 90 Minutes If:

  • You have multiple unrelated problem areas
  • You're dealing with complex or chronic conditions
  • You want full-body treatment
  • You need deep relaxation and stress relief
  • You prefer longer, more thorough sessions

Cancellation Policy

We understand that life happens and schedules change. To ensure we can accommodate all clients effectively, we have the following cancellation policy:

24-Hour Notice Required

Please provide at least 24 hours notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. This allows us to offer the time slot to another client.

Late Cancellations

Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the session cost.

No-Shows

Failure to attend your scheduled appointment without prior notice will result in a charge of 100% of the session cost.

To cancel or reschedule:

Call us at 0400 000 000 or email info@ninjamotiontherapies.com.au
